Stickball is a game played by several different native american ribes and it is a fun and exciting sport. There are still games played today with a very active playerbase. It is the origin of lacrosse which is played all over north america.
Snowsnake is a fun winter game played by tribes that live in a colder climate. There are different variations of the game but the main goal is the move the wooden snake or stick across the ice and snow by gliding it along the ground. Some snowsnakes are simple or intricately designed .
